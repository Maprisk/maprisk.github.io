Our Mission

MapRisk is designed to visualize geographical risks through interactive maps and data visualization. From hurricane tracking to flood awareness, we aim to make spatial data accessible, understandable, and useful for learners and professionals alike.

Data Sources

Demonstration maps currently use public data from OpenStreetMap and sample forecast geometry. Future updates will integrate real-time feeds from the National Hurricane Center (NOAA) and other meteorological sources.
